WELCOME TO V. K. PUBLIC SCHOOL

We believe in quality education!


V.K. Public School is a co-educational school that provides education to students from KG to Class 8th. The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), which is a prestigious education board in India. It is located in the Mahrajganj district of Uttar Pradesh, which is a rural area in northern India.

The school has a learner-centered approach to education and focuses on academic excellence, as well as humanism, knowledge, and compassion. The aim of the school is to provide students with an environment where they can develop their creative qualities and academic abilities in unique and entrepreneurial ways. At V.K. Public School, students are encouraged to express themselves and pursue their interests outside of the classroom.
